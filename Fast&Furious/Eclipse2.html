Fast & Furious (2001)

Driven by Brian O'Conner (Paul Walker), the 1995 Mitsubishi Eclipse GSX became an instant icon of street racing culture. Its distinctive neon green paint job and massive rear wing made it instantly recognizable.

"I live my life a quarter mile at a time."

While the movie version featured a 2JZ-GTE swap (a Toyota engine), the stock Eclipse came with a 4G63T. This modification allowed Brian to compete with the best on the streets of Los Angeles.

Engine Specs

Toyota 2JZ-GTE Inline-6 Turbo. Twin-turbo setup with custom intercooler and exhaust. Produces over 400 horsepower with proper tuning.

Chassis & Suspension

Reinforced chassis with adjustable coilovers, strut tower braces, and Brembo braking system for optimal handling and stopping power.

Aero & Visuals

Neon Green paint, large rear spoiler, underglow neon lighting system, and 18-inch alloy wheels with performance tires.
